## Summary

During the last three decades, a coiled tubing (CT) modeling software package has been continuously developed to assist in the planning and executing of global CT operations. The ﬁrst models were steady state. In the past decade, these models have been extended to consider transient eﬀects of operations as they are executed. The transient models are starting to be used to automate CT operations.
