## Summary

A digital twin of the above ground electro-mechanical and control systems for a drilling rig was created using a model-based software. Using a library of pre-built software models, reduced the development time of the digital twin using traditional software development methods from over a year with a team of experts, to less than 4 months for a single developer. In addition to a significant time savings, the resulting simulation was able to predict performance of the top drive, draw works and mud pumps with an error of less than 3% for most conditions.
