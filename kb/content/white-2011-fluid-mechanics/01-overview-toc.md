# Fluid Mechanics — Overview and Table of Contents

**Reference:** White, F.M. (2011). *Fluid Mechanics*, 7th Edition. McGraw-Hill. University of Rhode Island.

## Overview

White's *Fluid Mechanics* is a standard undergraduate/graduate engineering textbook covering the breadth of fluid mechanics from fundamental properties through advanced applications. The 7th edition spans 11 chapters plus appendices covering physical properties of fluids, compressible flow tables, conversion factors, and equations of motion in cylindrical coordinates.
